Never worry about deciding on a restaurant again!

The Problem

Adults make approximately 35,000 remotely conscious decisions each day. Research out of Cornell University has suggested that out of those thousands of daily decisions, people make at least 225 decisions each day on just food alone. It is upsetting that so many of these decisions have to be made in regards to something as fundamental and essential to our livelihood as food. When it comes to deciding where to enjoy a great meal, there are just way too many options out there, and many people share the same trait: indecisiveness. We end up wasting too much time and energy struggling to make a decision and even end up having unnecessary disputes with friends and partners as a result.

The Solution

Roll With It! makes restaurant suggestions based on a user’s personalized food profile which is built up on our mobile application and our customized die by harnessing the power of machine learning and artificial intelligence. We aim to eliminate the time wasted by countless college students every day in trying to decide on a restaurant to eat at, especially when going out with a group and having to choose an eatery which adheres to each individual’s needs.
